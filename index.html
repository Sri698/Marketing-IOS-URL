Atlas – Repertory

Atlas – Repertory is a homeopathy reference app designed for students, practitioners, and enthusiasts.

Key Features

  • Quick repertory-based medicine lookup
  • Easy-to-understand symptom mapping
  • Clean and distraction-free interface
  • Offline-friendly reference access

Who Is It For?

  • Homeopathy students
  • Medical practitioners
  • Researchers & learners

Why Atlas?

Atlas – Repertory helps users quickly find relevant homeopathic remedies using repertory logic, saving time and improving accuracy.
